## Minimum Coverage in Florida

- $10,000 Personal Injury Protection (PIP)
- $10,000 Property Damage Liability (PDL)

## What Is Not Required?

Florida does NOT require:
- Bodily injury liability (in most cases)

## Why Minimum Coverage Is Not Enough

Minimum coverage may leave you exposed financially.

<AffiliateCTA city="Florida" />

## Recommended Coverage

Experts suggest:
- $100K liability coverage
- Comprehensive insurance

## Final Advice

Always compare plans before choosing your policy.
